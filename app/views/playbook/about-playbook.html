About the playbook

This playbook is for people within the Future Farming and Countryside Programme (FFCP) at the Department for Environment, Food and Rural Affairs (Defra).

The playbook explains how we work within the Future Farming and Countryside Programme. You can find information about our:

  • users
  • ways of working
  • teams and how they’re set up

You can find cross-government guidance in the Government Digital Service (GDS) Service Manual and the GDS service standard.

The playbook does not include all information relating to the programme. For example, it’s not where you’ll find product-specific documentation or communications announcements.

You can:

  • give us your feedback to help improve the playbook
  • view our roadmap to see what we’re working on over the next few months.

Playbook proposition

Our proposition is to help people decide if any, all or part of their content should be in the playbook. It’s for:

  • people who work in the programme
  • people who are about to join the programme
  • external partners and ALBs
  • people interested in what the programme is doing and how it works

What should go in the playbook:

  • how we work in the programme
  • our culture and values
  • content that can be shared in public
  • content that can be understood by anyone - whether they work in the programme or are completely new to it - they do not need prior expert knowledge

What does not go in the playbook:

  • content designed for internal use only
  • internal documents (for example, governance documents)
  • short-term or temporary changes/ways of working in the programme
  • announcements, meeting dates or news
  • content that is better served elsewhere (duplicate content), such as the service hub
